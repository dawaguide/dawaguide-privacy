2. Location Access – Prominent Disclosure

🕌 Why we need your location:
Dawaguide uses your device's GPS / network location exclusively to calculate the five daily Islamic prayer times (Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha) and Qibla direction that are accurate for your geographic position. Without location access, prayer times cannot be calculated correctly.

Background Location

In order to keep prayer times accurate and deliver timely prayer-time notifications even when Dawaguide is not in the foreground, the app may access your location in the background. This background access is subject to the following safeguards:

  • A persistent foreground-service notification is always displayed whenever background location is active, so you are always aware.
  • You may revoke location permission at any time in your device Settings. Prayer times will then fall back to a manually entered city.
  • Location coordinates are used only in-device for calculation. They are never uploaded, stored on servers, or shared with any third party.
  • Background location is only requested after you explicitly grant permission through an in-app consent dialog that clearly explains its purpose.

⚠️ If you deny background location: The app will continue to function normally, but prayer-time notifications may be less accurate when your location changes. You can still use all other app features.

3. Persistent Notification

While location services are active in the background, Dawaguide displays a persistent foreground-service notification. This notification:

  • Informs you that location is being accessed to maintain accurate prayer times.
  • Remains visible for the entire duration background location is active.
  • Can be tapped to open the app and adjust location / notification settings.
  • Is dismissed automatically when the foreground service stops.

4. How We Use Information

  • Calculate and display accurate Islamic prayer times for your location.
  • Provide Qibla compass direction based on your coordinates.
  • Send optional prayer-time reminder notifications.
  • Diagnose app crashes and improve app performance.
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse of the app.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality described above.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.
  • Limited data (crash logs, performance metrics) may be shared with trusted third-party services strictly for app functionality:
    • Google Firebase – crash reporting & performance monitoring (Firebase Privacy Policy).
    • Google Play Services – location APIs (Google Privacy Policy).
  • Your precise location data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

6. Data Retention

Dawaguide does not maintain a user account system and does not store personal data on external servers. Any locally cached preferences (e.g., last known city) are stored only on your device and are removed when you uninstall the app.

7. Security

  • Industry-standard encryption is used for any network communication (HTTPS / TLS).
  • Location coordinates are processed entirely on-device and never leave your phone.
  • Access to any collected analytics data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

8. Children's Privacy

Dawaguide is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us so we can delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights & Choices

  • Revoke location permission: Go to device Settings → Apps → Dawaguide → Permissions → Location and change to "Only while using" or "Deny".
  • Disable notifications: Go to device Settings → Apps → Dawaguide → Notifications.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app immediately stops all data collection and removes all locally stored data.
  • Data requests: Contact us at the email below for any privacy-related request (access, deletion, correction).
